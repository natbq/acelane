Cookie Policy

What cookies we use and how to manage them.

Last updated: May 21, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and understand how the site is being used. Similar technologies (such as local storage, pixels, and tags) work the same way and are covered by this Policy.

2. How we use cookies

Ace Lane Logistics uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Make our website work and keep it secure.
  • Remember your preferences (for example, that you've already seen the cookie notice).
  • Understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

We do not use cookies to build advertising profiles or sell your information.

3. Categories of cookies we use

3.1 Strictly necessary

These are required for the site to function properly. They cannot be disabled. Examples include cookies that maintain security, store your cookie-notice acknowledgement, and submit forms.

3.2 Performance and analytics

These help us understand which pages are visited, how long people stay, and which content is most useful, so we can improve the site. They are set only if and where we deploy an analytics tool. Where required by law, these are loaded only after you give consent.

3.3 Functional

These remember choices you make (for example, your preferred view) so we can deliver a better experience.

5. Third-party services

Our site is hosted on Netlify, which may set technical cookies for hosting, security, and form submission. We may also embed content or use services (such as Google Fonts) that load resources from third-party domains; those providers may collect technical data such as IP addresses as part of delivering the resource. We do not control these third parties' privacy practices and recommend reviewing their notices for details.

6. Managing cookies

You can control cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: all major browsers let you block or delete cookies. Be aware that blocking strictly necessary cookies may break parts of the site.
  • Cookie banner: when applicable, our cookie banner provides an "acknowledge" action and a link to this Policy.
  • Do Not Track: we honor browser-level Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals where applicable.

For instructions on managing cookies in popular browsers, see:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

7. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the technologies we use or to the law. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
