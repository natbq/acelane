The Ace Standard

Freight reliability is built on process and the people behind it.

When communication fails and visibility is lost, operational gaps turn into delays, claims, and customer dissatisfaction. Our Verified Checkpoint System closes those gaps with vetted carriers and complete load management from booking to delivery. The result is a freight experience defined by Accountability, Clarity, and Execution.

    • Pickup and delivery locations (city/state or zip code)
    • Requested pickup date and delivery window or appointment
    • Commodity type, weight, piece count, and dimensions
    • Packaging type (palletized, crated, floor-loaded, loose cartons, or carts)
    • Special requirements (liftgate, white glove, temperature control, multi-stop, marshalling, etc.)

    Booking

    • Transit time, service level, and capacity locked in for dispatch
    • Live tracking link provided with real-time ETA updates during transit
    • All shipper references, requirements, and documentation confirmed before rolling
    • Active operating authority (MC/DOT) — minimum 12 months in operation
    • Cargo and liability insurance validated directly with provider ($100k minimum up to $250k cargo coverage)
    • FMCSA safety ratings reviewed and strict enforcement against carrier fraud
    • TSA, TWIC, and Hazmat certified drivers for specialized shipments

    Securing

    • Load requirements verified prior to dispatch (pallet jack, e-track, straps, side doors, and more)
    • Driver, unit, and carrier details included in order confirmation
    • Additional insurance and equipment photos available upon request
    • Live updates until driver is fully checked in with pick-up reference or paperwork in hand
    • Proactive communication to stay ahead of delays and avoid detention
    • Piece count and labels verified via photos; damages reported promptly
    • Reefer temperatures confirmed with data logs available on demand

    Departure

    • Freight secured and trailer space optimized for safety and load efficiency
    • Signed BOL with timestamps and photos shared for customer approval
    • Carrier agreements enforced with seal photos and strict penalties for any unauthorized breach
    • Cross-border documentation coordinated with customs broker for clearance into Canada or Mexico
    • Shipment tracked via MacroPoint and Samsara with real-time GPS and live road conditions
    • Driver check-ins throughout transit ensuring shipment integrity and immediate traffic updates
    • Dedicated 24/7 support on standby for reroutes, transloads, and any expedite needs

    Arrival

    • Load monitored through final approach, with immediate resolution of any issues
    • Delivery appointments scheduled and white glove services provided upon request
    • Driver checked in with appointment reference and shipper signed BOL
    • Shipment and seal integrity verified before offloading to the designated area
    • Inside delivery, dunnage, and debris removal handled per load and facility instructions
    • Onsite photos taken and timestamps confirmed by the receiver
    • Signed POD and delivery confirmation sent to the customer upon offload

    Closeout

    • Detention, layover, and lumper claims backed by ELD data for accuracy and transparency
    • All charges audited against the original quote and submitted for customer approval
    • Load invoiced automatically once all documentation is reviewed and complete
