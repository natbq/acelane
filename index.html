The Ace Standard

Poor communication is the most common and costly mistake in freight. Incomplete information and details that fall through the cracks can lead to delays, claims, and dissatisfied customers.

Our Verified Checkpoint System is a five-stage process that closes the gap by confirming and documenting each critical detail with clear communication at every shipping stage. The result is a freight experience defined by Accountability, Clarity, and Execution (ACE).

Step 1

We collect all critical shipment details upfront to match the right capacity and provide a competitive rate. No back-and-forth or delays.

    Shipment details:

  • Pickup and delivery locations (city/state or zip code)
  • Requested pickup date and delivery window or appointment
  • Commodity type, weight, piece count, and dimensions
  • Packaging type (palletized, crated, floor-loaded, loose cartons, or carts)
  • Special requirements (liftgate, white glove, temp control, trade show, multi-stop, etc.)

    Once approved:

  • Transit time, service level and capacity secured for immediate pickup
  • Pickup and delivery references, load-specific requirements and documentation confirmed
The details collected at this stage make all the difference, eliminating wasted time and keeping every shipment on schedule.
Step 2

Our network of qualified carriers covers North America, all tracked and managed from pickup to delivery.

    Carrier Verification:

  • Active operating authority (MC/DOT) confirmed, minimum 12 months in operation
  • Cargo and liability insurance validated directly with provider, $100k minimum up to $250k cargo coverage
  • Load requirements verified prior to dispatch (liftgate, e-track, straps, side doors, and more)
  • FMCSA safety ratings reviewed and strict compliance enforced against double brokering

    Carrier details:

  • Driver name, contact number, truck and trailer number provided
  • Live tracking link and real-time ETA updates from dispatch through delivery
  • TSA, TWIC, and Hazmat certified drivers available for specialized shipments
Additional insurance and equipment photos available upon request.
Step 3

Pickup details confirmed, appointments aligned, and driver dispatched before wheels roll.

We coordinate directly with your team and the carrier to make sure everything is in place before the driver arrives — no surprises, no missed windows.

  • Pickup appointment confirmed with shipper
  • Driver dispatched with complete load instructions
  • Pickup number and reference details shared
  • BOL and paperwork requirements communicated
  • Load confirmed picked up with check call

Once Approved

  • Pickup confirmed with timestamp and piece count
  • Shipment officially in transit, tracking initiated
Step 4

Proactive tracking and communication to address issues before they become problems.

We stay on top of your freight so you don't have to. Real-time visibility and check calls keep everyone aligned from pickup to delivery.

  • Regular check calls with driver throughout transit
  • Proactive ETA updates shared with consignee
  • Delays or exceptions communicated immediately
  • Appointment reconfirmed if schedule changes
  • Any claims or damage escalated and documented in real time

Once Approved

  • Freight confirmed on schedule and on track
  • Delivery appointment locked and consignee notified
Step 5

On-time delivery, POD confirmation, and follow-up to close every load cleanly and efficiently.

The job isn't done until the paperwork is. We confirm delivery, collect proof of delivery, and follow up to make sure everything went as planned.

  • Delivery confirmed with driver check call
  • Proof of delivery (POD) collected and filed
  • Any exceptions, shortages, or damages documented
  • Final invoice issued with all supporting documents
  • Customer follow-up to confirm satisfaction

Once Approved

  • POD signed and on file — load officially closed
  • Full audit trail available for any claims or disputes
