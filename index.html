Carrier Verification:

  • Active operating authority (MC/DOT) confirmed, minimum 12 months in operation
  • Cargo and liability insurance validated directly with provider, $100k minimum up to $250k cargo coverage
  • Load requirements verified prior to dispatch (liftgate, e-track, straps, side doors, and more)
  • FMCSA safety ratings reviewed and strict compliance enforced against double brokering
